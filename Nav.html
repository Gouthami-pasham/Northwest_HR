    Eligibility for Graduate Assistant
    Must be fully accepted in a graduate program at Northwest Missouri State University
    Must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 in 8 hours of graduate work OR
    2.75 GPA in undergraduate degree if enrolled in first trimester of graduate school
    Students are eligible for one graduate assistant position to support one graduate degree
    Maintain your Graduate Assistant position
    Must enroll and successfully complete 9 hours with minimum of 6 hours at the Graduate Level
    Must Maintain a 3.0 GPA each term and cumulative
